Frequently asked questions

What are the benefits of using essential oils?

Essential oils offer various benefits, including relaxation, stress relief, and aromatherapy for overall well-being.

How should I use essential oils?

You can use essential oils through diffusers, inhalation, or dilution for massages and skincare. Always follow recommended guidelines

Are essential oils safe for all skin types?

While generally safe, it's essential to perform a patch test and follow dilution guidelines, especially for those with sensitive skin.

Can essential oils be ingested?

It's advisable to consult with a healthcare professional before ingesting essential oils, as some may not be suitable for internal use.
